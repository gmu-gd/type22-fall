#### Exercise 9: Stop Motion
Make the best stop motion film starring a fruit or vegetable. You have 90 minutes. Submit your final video on Blackboard and upload it to our Vimeo group.

##### Technical Requirements
* Stop motion film
* Stars a fruit or vegetable
* Title card
* Audio if possible

##### Resources
You can easily sequence you photographs in Photoshop following [this tutorial](https://www.creativelysquared.com/article/how-to-turn-your-image-sequence-into-a-stop-motion-video).
